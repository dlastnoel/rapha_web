Your Medical Friend

About

The Study

Rapha is a medical history taking application that will help you manage your time, for faster transaction to doctors, to have an accurate and reliable medical data about your situation.

Rapha is a research capstone study by 4 students of the Don Mariano Marcos Memorial State University - Mid La Union Campus, College of Information Technology

Features

Multi-Platform

Rapha is a cross-platform program that runs on several operating systems. It can be accessed via browser or mobile device. Developing a responsive website and a mobile application with low system requirements make it easy for users to utilize it.

Analytics Ready

Rapha has a dashboard for its doctors. The dashboard contains the weekly appointments and the chief complaints of the patients. This enables easy monitoring for the doctors and knows what to expect from its patients.

Text Messaging Service

Rapha has a text messaging feature. It puts the user in touch with the doctor through text messages. Other tha that, it notifies the user about his/her appointment status.

Frequently Answered Questions

Account

To sign up for Rapha, all you need is a username and a phone number. Enter the One-Time Password (OTP) that was sent to your phone to confirm your number.

Use the username and phone number you used to sign up to get in. Type in the OTP that was sent to your inbox to move forward.

Using Rapha

To fill out the medical form, click the button that says "Enter Medical History." You can find the button on the main menu. Saving Medical History Record - By filling out the form, you can save a record of your health history.

At the end of filling out the form about your medical history. There's a message that says "Consult to a doctor?" If you click "yes," a list of doctors will appear. Choose the doctor you want. Pick a date and time based on the doctor's schedule.
